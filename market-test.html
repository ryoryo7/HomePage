Japan Market Test Program

Test whether your products can actually sell in Japan with minimal risk – without setting up a Japanese entity or signing exclusive distributor contracts.

Japan is one of the world's most attractive e-commerce markets with high purchasing power and a mature review culture. However, simple cross-border EC testing cannot accurately measure Japanese consumer behavior. Our Market Test Program uses Amazon Japan and Rakuten to collect real sales data under the same conditions that Japanese customers use daily.

Why You Need Real-Environment Testing

  • Cross-border EC has long delivery times and rarely generates reviews
  • No-return policies change purchase psychology, making demand measurement inaccurate
  • Cannot avoid Japanese regulations (Pharmaceutical Affairs Act, Premiums Act) or understand expression restrictions
  • Cannot adapt to Amazon Japan's unique SEO and advertising structure

Our program uses FBA and Japanese warehouses to replicate next-day delivery, Japanese customer service, and appropriate return policies. This allows you to get "real Japanese market reactions" even at the test stage.

What's Included

  • Product listing, page creation, and localization for Amazon Japan / Rakuten
  • Japanese copywriting, image optimization, and keyword research
  • Advertising operations (SP, SB, SD) with daily tuning
  • Review acquisition strategies
  • Japan-standard shipping via FBA or Japanese warehouse
  • Weekly reports and key KPI visualization
  • Entry decision materials created within 3-6 months

Data You'll Gain from Testing

Conversion Data

Measure actual CVR, cart acquisition rate, and review response to quantify your product's strength in the Japanese market.

Price & Profit Analysis

Understand viable price points for Japan and actual profit margins including advertising costs.

Competitive Analysis

Compare against competitors (reviews, pricing, ad intensity) to clarify your market position.

Winning Strategy Discovery

Determine data-backed whether a "winning formula" exists for your products in Japan.

Test Timeline and Process

We typically proceed with a 3-6 month test period.

Month 1

Setup and Launch

Create product pages, ship to FBA, configure advertising, and begin sales.

Month 2-4

Ad Optimization and Review Acquisition

Implement ad tuning, keyword optimization, and review acquisition strategies.

Month 5-6

Expansion Decision

Based on accumulated data, determine whether to proceed with full market entry and next steps.
