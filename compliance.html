Compliance + Brand Setup

Establish the foundation for selling in Japan, including regulatory checks, JAN codes, trademark registration, and Brand Registry.

The biggest barriers to the Japanese market are regulations and brand operations. Complex regulations exist for each category – Pharmaceutical Affairs Act, Premiums Act, PSE, technical standards, Food Sanitation Act – and sales suspensions due to labeling or advertising mistakes are not uncommon. Additionally, without JAN codes or trademarks, marketplace screening becomes difficult and brand protection is impossible.

We provide one-stop support for building the minimum necessary foundation to sell in Japan.

What's Included

Regulatory Compliance

  • Pharmaceutical Affairs Act, Premiums Act risk assessment
  • Electrical Appliance Safety Act (PSE) compliance check
  • Radio Act (technical standards) compliance check
  • Pre-check of advertising copy and product descriptions
  • Image labeling legality verification

Codes & Trademarks

  • JAN code acquisition support (GS1)
  • Trademark clearance preliminary research
  • Prior trademark verification
  • Japan trademark application support

Brand Protection

  • Amazon Brand Registry registration support
  • Anti-counterfeiting brand protection design
  • Unauthorized seller countermeasure advice

Packaging Compliance

  • Japan-specific packaging display improvements
  • Warning labels and ingredient list verification
  • Logo usage guideline development

Why Overseas Brands Stumble Here

  • Japanese regulations differ significantly from Western standards
  • No clear rules on what labeling is prohibited – "interpretation" is required
  • Direct translation of English ad copy can violate the Pharmaceutical Affairs Act
  • Without JAN codes and trademarks, Amazon screening gets stuck
  • Without Brand Registry, counterfeit protection is impossible

These areas require specialized knowledge, making them difficult for general EC agencies to handle. As a partner who understands both regulations and EC operations, we help you overcome these barriers.
